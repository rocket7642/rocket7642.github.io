Warframe Review

Buy the Game : Digital Extremes | Steam | Digital Trends | Playstation Store | Microsoft Store

Warframe is probably one of the fastest paced games I have played, up there with Titanfall (Both of them). It grabs your attention with the missions and slowly rewards you with new weapons as you play, never fully overwhelming you.

Pros~

  • Maps are randomly generated as to prevent the exact same experience each time
  • Many different enemy types with different resistances and different tilesets to fight them on
  • Many different warframes to play as, each giving you a very different play style
  • Quite a few different gameplay styles from fishing and harvesting to fluid melee gunfighting
  • Very passionate development studio that regularly produces videos and livestreams updates to the community
Cons~
  • A little bit overwhelming when you first start as it opens up quickly
  • Many things are locked behind cooperative work such as clans and raids
  • Two open world maps that have their whole own crafting resource system (might be a positive for some)
7/10 A great game but the grind may kill it for some people

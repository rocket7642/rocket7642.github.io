Terraria Review

Buy the Game : Relogic | Steam | Microsoft Store | Humble Bundle | Good Old Games | Nintendo Switch EStore

Terraria, one of the best games I have ran across to pass the time between eating and sleeping.

Pros~

  • Each world can take up to 4 weeks to do absolutly everything in
  • Massive amount of items and enemies
  • Diverse playstyles
  • Many different enviorments
  • Massive amount of mods available for extra customisation
  • Easy server use and availability
Cons~
  • If you only do the required, you can beat the game in 2 to 3 days
  • Sometimes can become frustrating if you become stuck
9/10 would recommend to others
